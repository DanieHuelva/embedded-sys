Maestro Servo Channel Map
Mini Maestro 18-Channel Servo Controller

Channel 0:
  Wheels – forward motion (drive)
  This channel controls the robot’s wheel movement.
  Observed forward/backward motion when tested.

Channel 1:
  Unused
  No movement observed during testing.
  Channel is not connected or not configured.

Channel 2:
  Waist rotation
  Controls rotation of the robot’s waist.

Channel 3:
  Head tilt
  Controls up/down movement of the robot’s head.

Channel 4:
  Head pan
  Controls left/right movement of the robot’s head.

Channel 5:
  Right arm – vertical (shoulder)
  Controls vertical movement of the right arm.

Channel 6:
  Right arm – horizontal
  Controls horizontal movement of the right arm.

Channel 7:
  Right arm – joint 3 (vertical)
  Controls vertical movement of the third joint on the right arm.

Channel 8:
  Right arm – joint 4 (vertical)
  Controls vertical movement of the fourth joint on the right arm.

Channel 9:
  Right hand – twist
  Controls rotational twisting of the right hand.

Channel 10:
  Right hand – grab
  Controls opening and closing of the right hand.

Channel 11:
  Left arm – joint 1 (vertical)
  Controls vertical movement of the first joint on the left arm.

Channel 12:
  Left arm – joint 2 (horizontal)
  Controls horizontal movement of the second joint on the left arm.

Channel 13:
  Left arm – joint 3 (vertical)
  Controls vertical movement of the third joint on the left arm.

Channel 14:
  Left arm – joint 4 (vertical)
  Controls vertical movement of the fourth joint on the left arm.

Channel 15:
  Left hand – twist
  Controls rotational twisting of the left hand.

Channel 16:
  Left hand – grab
  Controls opening and closing of the left hand.

Channel 17:
  Unused
  No servo connected to this channel.
